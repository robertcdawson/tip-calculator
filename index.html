How it Works

We calculate your tip based on your satisfaction and order type.

  • Order Type Matters:
    • Dine-in: Starts at 15%. Service is key.
    • Takeout: Starts at 5%. Food is key.
    • Delivery: Starts at 10%. Service is key.
  • Rating: 1 to 5 Stars. Higher ratings mean higher tips!

Example: Dine-in with 5-star service gets a great tip (~19%). Takeout with bad food gets a low tip (~2%).
